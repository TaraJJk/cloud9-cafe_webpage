Café Latte

Caffè latte, often shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk. A caffè latte is an espresso with milk. In Italy, a caffè is an espresso. Latte is milk. Ideally served with latte art on top and not too much foam.

Important

  • Increases energy level and performance for work or studying.
  • Lowers your risk of type 2 diabetes.
  • Eating your coffee as an alternative to hot coffee.
  • Coffee helps burn fat.
  • Less likely to develop Alzheimer’s disease.
