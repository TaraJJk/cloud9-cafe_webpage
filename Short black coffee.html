Short Black Coffee

Short black coffee is simply an espresso shot, with no added water except for water required to brew the coffee. We call it 'short' because we don't add any extra water; the name 'black' comes from the color of the coffee since there is no milk added.

In the western U.S., Italiano sometimes refers to a short Americano with equal amounts of espresso and water. The hot water can be drawn directly from the same espresso machine that is used to brew the espresso, or from a separate water heater or kettle.
